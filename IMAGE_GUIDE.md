- **Name it**: `profile.jpg` or `profile.png`

### Project Images
- **Location**: Projects page (`/projects`)
- **Recommended size**: 1200x800px (landscape) or 800x800px (square)
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WEBP
- **Name them**: `project1.png`, `project2.png`, etc.
  - Or use descriptive names like `ecommerce-platform.png`
